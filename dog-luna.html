Meet Luna

A beautiful and adventurous Husky Mix ready for exploration

About Luna

Luna is a stunning Husky Mix with a spirit of adventure and a heart full of love. Her striking blue eyes and beautiful coat make her stand out, but it's her personality that truly captures hearts.

Personality

Luna is intelligent, independent, and full of energy. She has the classic Husky traits - she's vocal, loves to run, and has a strong prey drive. Despite her independent nature, Luna is incredibly loyal to her family and forms deep bonds. She's also very social and gets along well with other dogs.

Likes & Dislikes

Loves

  • Long runs and hikes
  • Exploring new trails
  • Playing in the snow
  • Mental stimulation games
  • Meeting new people and dogs
  • Car rides to new places

Dislikes

  • Being confined for long periods
  • Hot weather
  • Bathing and grooming

Training & Behavior

Luna is intelligent and learns quickly, but she has a strong independent streak typical of Huskies. She's house-trained and knows basic commands. Luna needs consistent training and plenty of mental stimulation.

House Training: Fully trained
Basic Commands: Good
Recall: Needs work
Socialization: Excellent

Health & Care

Luna is in excellent health and receives regular veterinary care. She's up to date on all vaccinations and is spayed.

Vaccinations: Up to date
Spayed/Neutered: Yes
Microchipped: Yes
Health Issues: None
