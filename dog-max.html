Meet Max

A playful and energetic Labrador Retriever ready for adventure

About Max

Max is a fun-loving Labrador Retriever who brings joy and energy to every room he enters. His love for water and fetch makes him the perfect companion for active families.

Personality

Max is the quintessential Labrador - friendly, outgoing, and always ready for fun. He has an infectious enthusiasm that makes everyone around him smile. Max is incredibly loyal and forms strong bonds with his family members. He's also very intelligent and eager to learn new things.

Likes & Dislikes

Loves

  • Swimming in lakes and pools
  • Playing fetch with any toy
  • Long walks and hikes
  • Car rides and road trips
  • Training sessions and treats
  • Meeting new people and dogs

Dislikes

  • Being left alone
  • Loud noises
  • Having his ears cleaned

Training & Behavior

Max is well-trained and responds excellently to commands. He's house-trained and crate-trained. Max has a natural retrieving instinct and loves to work. He's great with children and other pets.

House Training: Fully trained
Basic Commands: Mastered
Retrieving: Natural instinct
Socialization: Excellent

Health & Care

Max is in excellent health and receives regular veterinary care. He's up to date on all vaccinations and is neutered.

Vaccinations: Up to date
Spayed/Neutered: Yes
Microchipped: Yes
Health Issues: None
