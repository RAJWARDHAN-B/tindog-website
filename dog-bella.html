Meet Bella

A loving Golden Retriever looking for her forever companion

About Bella

Bella is a sweet and energetic Golden Retriever who loves nothing more than spending time with her humans and going on adventures.

Personality

Bella is the epitome of a Golden Retriever - friendly, intelligent, and eager to please. She has a gentle temperament and gets along well with everyone she meets, including children and other dogs. Her playful nature makes her the perfect companion for active families.

Likes & Dislikes

Loves

  • Long walks in the park
  • Playing fetch with tennis balls
  • Swimming in lakes and pools
  • Car rides and adventures
  • Training sessions and treats
  • Cuddling on the couch

Dislikes

  • Being left alone for long periods
  • Loud thunderstorms
  • Having her nails trimmed

Training & Behavior

Bella is well-trained and responds to basic commands including sit, stay, come, and heel. She's house-trained and crate-trained. Bella walks well on a leash and is generally well-behaved in public settings.

House Training: Fully trained
Basic Commands: Mastered
Leash Walking: Excellent
Socialization: Very social

Health & Care

Bella is in excellent health and receives regular veterinary care. She's up to date on all vaccinations and is spayed.

Vaccinations: Up to date
Spayed/Neutered: Yes
Microchipped: Yes
Health Issues: None
