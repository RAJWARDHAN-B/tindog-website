About TinDog

The world's first dating app designed specifically for dogs and their owners.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, TinDog was born from a simple observation: dogs deserve love too! Our founder, a passionate dog lover, noticed that while there were plenty of dating apps for humans, our four-legged friends were left to find love through chance encounters at the dog park.

Our Mission

To connect dogs and their owners with compatible matches, creating happy families and wagging tails across the world.

Our Values

Safety, authenticity, and the belief that every dog deserves to find their perfect companion.

Our Community

Over 1 million happy dogs and owners who have found love through our platform.
