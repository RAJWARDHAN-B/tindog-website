Getting Started

Creating a profile is easy! Download the TinDog app, sign up with your email, and then add your dog's information including photos, breed, age, personality traits, and what you're looking for in a playmate. You can also add multiple dogs to your account.

Yes! TinDog offers a free basic plan that allows you to create profiles, browse other dogs, and send a limited number of messages per day. We also offer premium plans with additional features like unlimited messaging, advanced filters, and priority support.

Dogs should be at least 6 months old and fully vaccinated before using TinDog. This ensures they're ready for social interactions and helps maintain a safe environment for all users. Puppies under 6 months are still developing their social skills and may not be ready for playdates.

App Features

Our matching algorithm considers factors like breed compatibility, age, energy level, location, and owner preferences. We use advanced machine learning to suggest the best potential matches for your dog. You can also set specific filters to narrow down your search.

Yes! Once you've matched with another dog owner, you can use our built-in scheduling feature to coordinate playdates. You can suggest meeting times, locations, and activities. The app also sends reminders and allows you to share photos from your meetups.

Not every match will be perfect, and that's okay! You can unmatch with dogs that aren't a good fit. We also provide tips for introducing dogs safely and encourage owners to meet in neutral locations first. Your dog's safety and comfort are our top priorities.

Safety & Privacy

We use multiple verification methods including email verification, phone number verification, and social media linking. We also have a reporting system for inappropriate behavior and employ AI-powered content moderation. All users must agree to our community guidelines.

Absolutely! We take privacy seriously and never share your personal information with third parties. Your location is only shared with matches you approve, and you can control what information is visible on your profile. We use industry-standard encryption to protect all data.

If you encounter suspicious behavior, immediately report it through the app's reporting feature. You can also block users and unmatch with them. Our safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours and takes appropriate action. Never share personal information or meet someone who makes you uncomfortable.

Billing & Premium

Premium plans include unlimited messaging, advanced search filters, priority customer support, read receipts, and the ability to see who liked your profile. We also offer a premium plus plan with additional features like profile boosts and advanced analytics.

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. We don't charge cancellation fees.

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new premium subscribers. If you're not satisfied with your premium experience within the first week, contact our support team for a full refund. After the trial period, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.
