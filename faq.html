Getting Started

Creating a profile is easy! Download the TinDog app, sign up with your email, and then fill out your dog's profile with photos, breed, age, personality traits, and what you're looking for in a match.

TinDog offers a free tier with limited features and 5 matches per day. We also have premium plans (Labrador $49/month and Mastiff $99/month) that offer unlimited matches and additional features.
